Hydraulic and Lube Filters

• Hy-Pro DFE Rated Elements for Improved ISO Cleanliness and Longer Service Life
• Upgrade Replacement Elements for ALL Brands, including Pall, Parker, Hydac, MP,    Internorman, Taise Kogyo, and Stauff.

Improving fluid cleanliness equates to longer fluid life, increased reliability of hydraulic equipment, and a reduction in costly service and repairs. Filters For Industry, Inc. offers Hy-Pro DFE rated elements that are proven to deliver better ISO cleanliness codes and longer service life than many other like products on the market. We offer a full line of replacement elements for all brands including, but not limited to, Pall, Parker, Hydac, MP, Internorman, Taise Kogyo, and Stauff. Filters For Industry, Inc. also supplies Hy-Pro filter housings and assemblies capable of withstanding pressures up to 6000 PSI for applications ranging from high flow return-line filtration to off-line system flushing.
