Our customer base has grown throughout the southern United States to include some of the largest firms in Pulp and Paper, Power Generation, Primary Metals, Chemical Process, and the Automotive Industry.

Filters For Industry, Inc. is a full-line distributor and manufacturer of filtration products and filtering systems.
