What we do

Filtration Solutions

Filters For Industry, Inc. is a full-line distributor and manufacturer of filtration products and systems. Since 1985, we have provided cost-effective filtration solutions for pulp and paper, power generation, primary metals, chemical process, and the automotive industries.

Our exemplary customer service, large product inventories, and knowledgable technical support have cemented our reputation as a top supplier within the industry. Filtration problems? Solutions are just a phone call away.
